What is Trade Buddy?

Welcome to Trade Buddy. We are a platform that allows anyone to trade anything, ranging from books, clothing, electronics, and much more.

Do I have to pay?

Using Trade Buddy is completely free. However, free users can only list 1 item for 1 month. Subscription plans for extended listings are coming soon.

Is your code open source?

All of the code for our website is open source on GitHub.
