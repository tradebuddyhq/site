Our Story

Trade Buddy was founded with the mission to build a sustainable and community-driven marketplace. We believe in reducing waste by giving pre-loved items a new home through meaningful trade.

From a humble idea, we grew into a trusted local platform that encourages simple, safe, and rewarding exchanges. Our team is committed to making trading easy and accessible for everyone.
